# Pet-Care-Sim
A modern Tamagotchi-style virtual pet game for smartphones.

## Core Features
- Raise and care for virtual pets with unique personalities
- Feed, play, clean, and interact with your pet daily
- Watch your pet grow and evolve based on care quality
- Unlock new pets, apparel, decorations / furniture, and activities over time

## Game Ideas

### Pet Care & Interaction
1. Pedometer integration to incentivize real-world exercise for in-game bonuses
2. Mini-games (puzzle, reflex, memory) to play with your pet
3. Pet emotions and mood system affected by care and weather
4. Customizable pet appearance (accessories, outfits, colors)
5. Voice interaction - talk to your pet using microphone
6. AR mode to see your pet in the real world via camera

### Pet Variety
1. Huge variety of pets: realistic animals, fantasy creatures, and comedic characters
2. Rare and legendary pets to collect
3. Hybrid breeding system to create unique pet combinations
4. Seasonal and event-exclusive pets

### Social Features
1. Local network visiting - visit friends' pets nearby
2. Mail system to send gifts and messages
3. Online leaderboards and achievements
4. Pet playdates and competitions
5. Trading system for items and pet eggs
6. Global events and challenges

### Progression & Customization
1. Level up system with skill trees
2. Customize your pet's home with furniture and decorations
3. Unlock new locations and environments
4. Career paths for pets (athlete, artist, scholar, etc.)
5. Photo mode to capture and share moments
6. Daily quests and weekly challenges

### Engagement Features
1. Push notifications for pet needs (not intrusive)
2. Widget support to check pet status from home screen
3. Offline care system - pets don't die when you're away
4. Time-based events and surprises
5. Seasonal themes and holiday celebrations
6. Cloud save to prevent losing progress
